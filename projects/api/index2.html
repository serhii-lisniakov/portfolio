About

WellnessDetector™ is a non-contact, anonymous, vital sign screening device using state of the art sensors and machine learning to non-invasively determine an individual's state of health. A person's temperature, heart and lung function are measured in real-time, from 1-3 meters away. This data is then utilized by the system to understand whether a person is healthy or unwell (incl. Covid-19). Applications for this device range from, protecting nurses/first responders by allowing them to screen vital signs at safe distances, to prison population health monitoring, to screening employees/visitors at business entrances, along with many others.
